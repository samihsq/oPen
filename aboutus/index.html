About Us

oPen was created by high schooler Samih Qureshi, part of the class of 2023. He was a cofounder of the non-profit WeLovePi, which provides robotics education for underprivileged students in Title I schools. Along with this, over the pandemic, he assisted senior citizens in local senior centers with their technological issues. He was also a cofounder of an award-winning robotics team, along with being the president of the Washington High School Robotics Club. He has advocated for retirement benefits for police K9s at the California state legislature, and has been an advocate for environment and wildlife conservation.
